Ingredients for Jeremy's Curry Chicken

My personal flair for a simple home-cooked Chicken Curry

  • Cut chicken bits 700-800 grams.
  • 2 Onions.
  • Frozen or fresh peas 200-300 grams.
  • Heavy Cream 3 dl.
  • Sesame or chia seeds 2-4 tbsp.
  • Chicken bouillion cubes 1-2
  • Curry Seasoning 3-4 tbsp.
  • Cumin powder 1 tbsp.
  • Pepper mix 4-5 tbsp.
  • Ketchup or sugar for extra sweetness 2-4 tbsp.
  • Mild mustard 2 tbsp.
  • Salt for taste.
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil.

Steps

  1. Dice and fry the onions in olive oil until soft and golden.
  2. Add the seeds and fry them along the onions for a bit, add olive oil if onions have absorbed most of the oil.
  3. Turn down the heat to medium and add the curry powder and let it sizzle a while.
  4. Turn up the heat to fry the chicken until golden.
  5. Add the heavy cream.
  6. Add the cumin powder and pepper seasonings and mix well.
  7. Add the sweetness with sugar or ketchup and the mustard.
  8. Add the bouillion cubes and mix well.
  9. Salt to taste and simmer on low heat for 40-60 minutes
  10. Enjoy with your favorite carbs. Me and my partner prefer pasta, but rice is usually the go-to with curry.
