Ingredients for Jeremy's Classic Finnish minced-beef sauce

Classic Finnish cuisine simplified with personal taste.

  • Minced Beef (10-23% fat content depending on preference) 700-800 grams
  • 2 Onions
  • Heavy Cream 3dl
  • Garlic or garlic powder 2 tbsp.
  • Pepper mix 2 tbsp.
  • Minced Beef seasoning 2tbsp.
  • Beef Bouillion cubes 1-2 depending on taste
  • Ketchup 4 tbsp.
  • Mild Mustard for thickening 3 tbsp.
  • Salt in a pinch depending on taste after stock cubes
  • Virgin Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Dice and fry the onions and chilis in olive oil until soft and golden.
  2. Add the minced beef and fry until brown.
  3. Add the heavy cream.
  4. Add the seasonings and bouillion cubes.
  5. Add ketchup and mustard.
  6. Salt to your tastes.
  7. Simmer on low heat for 15 - 20 minutes.
  8. Enjoy with preferred carbs. Rice, pasta, potatoes and potato mash are the usual for this Finnish classic home cooked meal.
